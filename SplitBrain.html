Split Brain

A split-screen arcade game that trains both sides of your brain by making you control two characters at the same time.
Dual-thumb control Split-screen gameplay Skill & focus Power-ups

One character climbs up by landing on platforms, while the other descends by finding gaps. The challenge is coordinating both sides at once — simple to learn, hard to master.

Built for quick sessions, smooth difficulty ramp, and that “one more run” feeling.
